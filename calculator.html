RV Financing Tips

Down Payment

A larger down payment (20% or more) can help you get better interest rates, lower monthly payments, and potentially avoid being upside-down on your loan.

Interest Rates

RV loan rates vary by credit score, loan term, and RV age. New RVs typically get better rates (4-7%) than used RVs. Shop around with multiple lenders.

Hidden Costs

Don't forget: registration fees, campground memberships, fuel costs, maintenance, winterization, and unexpected repairs. Budget for the unexpected!
