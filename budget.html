Budgeting Tips for RV Living

Track Everything

Keep receipts and track actual spending for 2-3 months. Your estimates may not match reality. Adjust your budget based on real data.

Build an Emergency Fund

RVs need repairs. Aim for $3,000-5,000 emergency fund minimum. Unexpected breakdowns happen - be prepared!

Free Camping Options

Use apps like iOverlander, FreeRoam, and Campendium to find free camping spots (BLM land, national forests, Walmart parking, etc.)
